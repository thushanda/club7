Club 7's first trophy triumph came in the FA Cup back in 1903-04, with two more victories taken in before Mercer added to his impressive collection of honours in 1968-69. The competition also allowed the Blues to get back in a winning habit following the arrival of Sheikh Mansour, with Yaya Toure netting an emotional match winner against Stoke in 2011.
